Primary Colors
Blue (#2563EB – Tailwind blue-600): Trust, reliability

White (#FFFFFF): Clean, open layout

Slate (#64748B – Tailwind slate-500/600): Professional and neutral

✅ Accent Colors
Green (#10B981 – Tailwind emerald-500): Positive actions (like “debt cleared”)

Red (#EF4444 – Tailwind red-500): For warnings or overdue payments, use sparingly

Amber (#F59E0B – Tailwind amber-500): Reminders or upcoming due dates

✅ Backgrounds & Neutrals
Gray-100 / Gray-50: For light background sections

Gray-800: For dark mode or footer sections
